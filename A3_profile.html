Group Process:

Throughout the course of assignment 2 our group had many triumphs and shortcomings when it came to producing the body of work we submitted. We worked well in a team environment as we all were on the same page and never argued over the delegation of roles or the work that was being produced by each individual member. Everyone was consistent in their work ethic and were very flexible when it came to spending hours outside of class to meet up and work together for extended periods of time. However our team did have its shortcomings, we started very late on the project and lacked initiative to speak up and organise the chaos that was our early teamwork. We often had to spend many hours together in person to complete the assignment by the due date. Leaving many of us tired and exhausted mentally. Going forward our group will be a lot more eager and strict to ensure work gets done on time and in an organised fashion to eliminate the problem of being behind, which in turn also helps the team members to relax more and produce even greater work and work ethic than before. We will also continue to regularly meet up in person to enhance our understanding of not only each other but our project as a whole, allowing us to properly convey our ideas and thoughts to better refine the current foundation of our project.
